3D Printing Workshop




31st October,2015




Workshop in association with NSSC(National Students’ Space Challenge)




Event Overview

The 3D printing workshop was conducted by Kharagpur Open Source Society, in association with NSSC (National Students’ Space Challenge), powered by Srijan Creations, a Kolkata based 3D Printing institute.
The live demostrations were simply fascinating. There was also an exhibition at the end with some unique masterpiece designs to show around. We thank Srijan Creations for taking time and coming for the informatory seminar.
