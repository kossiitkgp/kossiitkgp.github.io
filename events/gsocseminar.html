Google Summer Of Code

About the Seminar

GSOC seminar is an interactive session organised by KOSS for the first year and second year students to brief them about the annual GSOC programme. Students who participated in GSOC as mentors or mentees share their experiences.

This seminar also helps in solving many queries and FAQs of the inquisitive coders. Also a live demo of using Git and selecting organisations is given by the seniors which helps in boosting the confidence of the young ones.
